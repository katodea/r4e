recurring events

yep. these are our regular events. they're weather-dependent so check our instagram or slack if the forecast is crummy.

monday night rides

  • duration: two to three hours, depending on how long we spend hanging out at the halfway point.
  • pace: no drop, party pace.
  • rsvp required? no.
  • departs: every monday at 5:30pm from oregon hill overlook.

thursday night rides

  • duration: about two hours.
  • pace: no drop, party pace.
  • rsvp required? nope.
  • departs: every thursday at 5:30pm from shiplock park.

these are the bigger things we've got planned! if you're interested in attending or helping out, get in touch.

upcoming special events

the great queer bike parade 2022

yes! gay! bike! parade!

we host this bike-powered pride parade every year as a way to celebrate the queer biking community. participants are encouraged to don their favorite, gayest outfit, decorate their bikes, and get ready for a party on wheels.

  • duration: about two hours.
  • pace: slow. it's a parade!
  • rsvp required? nope.
  • departs: sunday, may 29th 2022 at 2pm from monroe park.

we'll be grilling and hanging out in smith peters park after (the parade will end there around 4pm). hope to see you there!
